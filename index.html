Al-Khwarizmi

Mathematician and Astronomer

Born in 1780

Al-Khwārizmī lived in Baghdad, where he worked at the “House of Wisdom” (Dār al-Ḥikma) under the caliphate of al-Maʾmūn.The House of Wisdom acquired and translated scientific and philosophic treatises, particularly Greek, as well as publishing original research.

Khwarazm or Chorasmia is a large oasis region on the Amu Darya river delta in western Central Asia, bordered on the north by the Aral Sea, on the east by the Kyzylkum Desert, on the south by the Karakum Desert, and on the west by the Ustyurt Plateau
